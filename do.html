Things To Do

Cincinnati has some of the funnest most exciting attractions in southern Ohio! There are plenty of festivals and activitys to dive into.

Festivals

Oktoberfest

Celebrate a festival that celibrates Germany history. During this two day event filled with beer and music you are sure to be enriched in German culture.

PNC Festival of Lights

Vist the Cincinnati Zoo as they host their annual light show that showcases some of the most beautiful light deisplays around!

Riverfest

Riverfest is Cincinnati's largest bash held over labor-day weekend. This event has a spectacuar firework show and is famous for their rubber duck races.
