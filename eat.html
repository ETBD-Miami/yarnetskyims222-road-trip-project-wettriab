Places To Eat

Cincinnati has some of the greatest places to eat in Ohio on this webpage I will be listing five of my favoirte restraunts that I have visited over the years for both breakfast and lunch/dinner.

Breakfast Top Five

Maplewood Kitchen and Bar

Spacious cafe for Californian cuisine for breakfast through dinner amid wooden tables & greenery.

Holtmans Donuts

Some of the most delicious donuts that your taste buds will ever have the pleasure of encountering.

Sleepy Bee Cafe

One of the coziest restraunts you will step foot in. This restraunt takes pride in getting their ingedients locally sourced!

Sugar and Spice Restraunt

Home of the orginal whispy thin pancakes that will make you come back wanting more.

Taste of Belgium

Words cannot describe how tasty and flavorful the waffles here are. It is a must try.

Lunch and Dinner Top Five

The Eagle

Located in the Over The Rhine District of Cincinnati this restraunts offers some of the best fried chicken you will ever eat. Not to mention the mothwatering comort food that goes along with it.

Sotto

Hands down the best authenic Itlaian crusine you can find in Cincinnati.

The Bakers Table

A beauftifull cafe and bakery that offers locally sourced crusine with enough variety to satisfy the enitre family.

Copper and Flame

With over seventy beers on tap this bar restraunt hybrid offers a relazing place for any hardworker to relax after a hard-days work.

Skyline Chili
